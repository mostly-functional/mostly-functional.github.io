Regular expressions (often abbreviated to regex or regex) is a language for matching strings – not a programming language. Still, anybody who does programming or scripting will regularly run into situations where regexes are the best way to solve a problem.

Learning how to write regexes is straightforward and shouldn’t take long, although regexes in the wild can often become very complex.
